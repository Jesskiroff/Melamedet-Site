Sefer Shemuel ~ ספר שמואל

Shemuel HaNavi wrote sefer Shoftim, Sefer Shemuel, and Megillat Rut.

Being that he wrote Sefer Shemuel, one would expect the sefer to be about the Prophet. It is fascinating to learn that the sefer focuses, as a matter or fact, on kings, namely, David Hamelech.

We learn our hilchot tefillah from shemuel א

Sefer Shemuel starts off with his mother's prayers. She yearned for a child and prayed her heart out to have one. Interestingly enough, this book heavily focuses on David Hamelech, who is known to be כולו תפילה .

David Hamelech wrote most of Tehillim

Tehillim, or the Book of Psalms, is one of the 24 books of tanach and is composed of poetry from some of our most prominent Tanach characters. These characters each cried out to Hashem, either praising him or reaching out in ultra-belief that he will bring them their salvation. David Hamelech had a rather difficult life, which he used as a vehicle of building his relationship with Hashem.
